Why I like Reading-

Reading transports me to different worlds and introduces me to fascinating characters. I enjoy diving into a variety of genres, from Fantasy to slow-burn, Mon-Rom,dark romance.Each book I read broadens my imagination and deepens my understanding of different cultures and experiences. I love the feeling of getting lost in a story, where time seems to disappear, and I can explore new ideas and perspectives. Reading not only entertains me but also inspires my creativity and fuels my curiosity about the world around me. Through the pages of a book, I find endless adventures waiting to be
