Why I do Art-

Making art taps into my creativity, allowing me to think outside the box and experiment with different techniques, materials, and styles. Art also serves as a therapeutic outlet, helping me relax, reduce stress, and process complex emotions in a calming, meditative way. The sense of accomplishment I feel when completing a piece, along with the opportunity to learn and grow through the creative process, makes it incredibly fulfilling. Additionally, art allows me to escape reality and focus entirely on the present moment, offering a break from everyday worries. Sharing my work with others fosters connections, and sometimes, my art becomes a personal reflection, revealing insights about my own experiences and growth. Ultimately, making art is a powerful combination of self-expression, creativity, and personal discovery that I find deeply satisfying.
